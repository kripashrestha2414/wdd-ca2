Sustainable Fashion

There are many brands that are commited to sustainablilty. Here are a few reputatble brands and organsiations.

1. KOTN is a Canadian based fashion brand that soucres it cotten from Egypt. Founded by three friends Ben, Mackenzie, and Rami. Inspired by Rami's experinces during his trip back to his family home in Eqypt and livnig in a cotten farming community in the Nile-Delta for six months. KOTN sources there materials from local egyptian cotten farms, uses a pricing method that provides the farmers with compensation that is 35% above the market pay, provides subsidies to the farmers during periods of difficult market conditions and also helps fund and build primary schools in the Nile-Delta regions by partnering with local NGO's and donating a portion of proceeds from each order they receive. To date they have built 21 schools.

2. Blooh is a US based kenyian jewelry brand. The jewlrey is sourced and handmade in workshops in Naibrobi,Kenya as well as vetteed global partners who are just as commited to Blooh's priority of worker wellbeing, fair wages and sustainablilty. They partner with small local businesses to limit resources like water consumption during production process. All the material is locally sourced and products are made to order to limit waste. To reduce plastic waste, shipping packaging is made of recyclable and compostable materials.From humble beginings of partnering with just local artisans in Nairobi in 2021, they have slowly grown to multiple local and global workshops. Empowering local communities by allowing artisans to set their own rates.

3. Cuyana is a US based handbag brand.Their products are 100% made from sustainably-certified materials.They are commited to responsible production, selling through 90% of the products they make. The bags are handmade and all materials are responsibly sourced.They partner with craftsmen and women globaly, from Ecuador to Italy. Their products are made with care and made to last.

4. Pact is a US based clothing brand. They partner with Fair Trade USA, Global Organic Textile Standard and SimpliZero. They ensure that their factories provide safe working conditions. Their shipping materials are made from recycled paper.Their organic cotton is sourced from India, with most of their manufacturing happening in worlds first fair trade-certified factory in Kolkata,India. Pact pays over 1 million dollers in fair trade premiums to ensure safe working conditions and additional development funds to on-site workers.
